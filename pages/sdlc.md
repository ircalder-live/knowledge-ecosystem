# Systems Development Life Cycle (SDLC) / 系统开发生命周期

## English Definition
A structured process for planning, creating, testing, deploying, and maintaining information systems.

## Chinese Definition
用于规划、创建、测试、部署和维护信息系统的结构化过程。

## Pronunciation
xìtǒng kāifā shēngmìng zhōuqī

## Conceptual Notes
The SDLC provides a disciplined framework that ensures systems are built systematically, with clear phases and checkpoints.

## Cultural Anchor
类似于中国传统工程项目中的“阶段性推进”，强调在每个阶段完成明确任务后再进入下一阶段。

## Related Terms
- Systems Analysis / 系统分析
- Systems Design / 系统设计

## Teaching Notes
- Emphasize the phased nature of SDLC and how it supports governance and quality.
- Use the cultural anchor to connect SDLC with familiar project rhythms.
- Encourage students to compare Western SDLC models with Chinese phased project traditions.
