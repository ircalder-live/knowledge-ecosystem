# Systems Design / 系统设计

## English Definition
Creating or modifying a system so that its components work together effectively to meet goals.

## Chinese Definition
创建或修改一个系统，使其各组成部分能够有效协同工作以实现目标。

## Pronunciation
xìtǒng shèjì

## Conceptual Notes
Design focuses on constructing or improving the system after analysis has clarified its structure and requirements.

## Cultural Anchor
类似于中国传统工匠精神中的“精益求精”，强调在理解整体的基础上进行精心构建。

## Related Terms
- Systems Analysis / 系统分析
- SDLC / 系统开发生命周期

## Teaching Notes
- Emphasize the transition from analysis (understanding) to design (constructing).
- Use the cultural anchor to connect design with craftsmanship and intentionality.
- Encourage students to compare Western design methodologies with Chinese holistic design traditions.
