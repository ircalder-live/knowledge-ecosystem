# Privacy / 隐私

## Overview / 概述
Privacy refers to an individual's right to control their personal information,
including how it is collected, used, stored, and shared. It protects personal
boundaries and supports autonomy in digital environments.

隐私指个人对其个人信息的控制权，包括信息如何被收集、使用、存储和共享。
隐私保护个人边界，并在数字环境中支持个人自主性。

---

## Why Privacy Matters / 隐私的重要性
Privacy is foundational to Digital Ethics because it affects:

- personal dignity  
- autonomy and choice  
- trust in digital systems  
- safety and security  
- social participation  
- digital identity  

隐私是数字伦理的基础，因为它影响：

- 个人尊严  
- 自主性与选择  
- 对数字系统的信任  
- 安全与保障  
- 社会参与  
- 数字身份  

---

## Key Ideas / 关键概念

### **Data Collection / 数据收集**
Understanding what information is gathered and why.  
理解收集了哪些信息以及原因。

### **Data Use / 数据使用**
Ensuring information is used appropriately and ethically.  
确保信息被适当且合乎伦理地使用。

### **Data Storage / 数据存储**
Protecting information from unauthorized access or misuse.  
保护信息免受未经授权的访问或滥用。

### **Data Sharing / 数据共享**
Controlling who can access or distribute personal information.  
控制谁可以访问或分发个人信息。

## Cultural Anchors / 文化锚点
- Western contexts emphasize **individual rights**, autonomy, and consent.  
- Chinese contexts emphasize **dignity**, respect, and social harmony (和谐).

- 西方语境强调**个人权利**、自主性和同意。  
- 中国语境强调**尊严**、尊重和社会和谐（和谐）。

These perspectives complement each other and enrich bilingual teaching.

这些视角相互补充，有助于双语教学。

---

## Teaching Notes / 教学提示
- Use real‑world examples (apps, websites, social platforms).  
- Highlight differences between legal compliance and ethical responsibility.  
- Encourage learners to compare cultural expectations around privacy.  
- Connect privacy to related concepts such as consent and transparency.

- 使用真实案例（应用程序、网站、社交平台）。  
- 强调法律合规与伦理责任的区别。  
- 鼓励学习者比较不同文化中的隐私期望。  
- 将隐私与同意、透明性等相关概念联系起来。
