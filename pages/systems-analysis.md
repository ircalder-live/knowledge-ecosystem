# Systems Analysis / 系统分析

## English Definition
Studying a system to understand its components and how they work together to meet goals.

## Chinese Definition
研究一个系统，了解其组成部分以及它们如何协同工作以实现目标。

## Pronunciation
xìtǒng fēnxī

## Conceptual Notes
Analysis involves breaking something into parts to understand the whole.

## Cultural Anchor
像《孙子兵法》中的‘知己知彼’——全面了解敌我情况，才能制定策略。

## Related Terms
- Systems Design / 系统设计
- SDLC / 系统开发生命周期

## Teaching Notes
- Emphasize the “breaking down to understand the whole” concept.
- Use the cultural anchor to connect abstract analysis to familiar strategic thinking.
- Encourage students to compare Western “analysis” with Chinese holistic traditions.
