# Research Workflow Template

## 1. Problem Definition
- Describe the research question or problem.
- Identify related issues or dependencies.

## 2. Background and Context
- Summarize relevant prior work, theory, or domain knowledge.

## 3. Experimental Design
- Define the planned experiment(s).
- Identify variables, parameters, and expected outcomes.

## 4. Data and Materials
- Specify datasets, sources, or materials required.
- Note any ethical or governance considerations.

## 5. Execution Plan
- Step-by-step procedure for conducting the research.
- Tools, environments, or platforms required.

## 6. Logging and Documentation
- Reference the experiment log template.
- Define what must be recorded during execution.

## 7. Analysis Plan
- Describe how results will be interpreted.
- Identify metrics, evaluation methods, or comparison baselines.

## 8. Risks and Mitigations
- Identify potential issues and how to address them.

## 9. Expected Outputs
- List expected artifacts (logs, analyses, domain outputs).

## 10. Next Steps and Integration
- Describe how this workflow connects to downstream tasks.
